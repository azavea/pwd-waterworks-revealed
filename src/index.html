Welcome to the Fairmount Water Works' "Urban Water Revealed Prototype app." This work in progress is meant to give Philadelphians and visitors to our city self-guided access to the grounds of the historic Water Works complex through space and time.

Please traverse our landscape to discover viewing points as portals that will take you through time to better understand the 19th century landmark innovation before you and its continued significance in the global water story.

Enjoy!

Karen Young — Executive Director, The Fairmount Water Works
